# Flight Delays, Cancellations, and Disruptions

**Document ID:** POL-DSR-001
**Last updated:** 2026-02-18

## Delays

For flights delayed at the departure airport, SkyLine Airways provides the following assistance:

- **2+ hours (short-haul):** Meal vouchers and refreshments
- **3+ hours (medium-haul):** Meal vouchers and refreshments
- **4+ hours (long-haul):** Meal vouchers, refreshments, and where applicable, hotel accommodation
- **Overnight delay:** Hotel accommodation and transport between airport and hotel

## Compensation under UK261 / EU261

Passengers may be entitled to compensation when arrival is delayed by 3 or more hours, provided the delay is not caused by extraordinary circumstances:

- **Short-haul (under 1,500km):** GBP 220
- **Medium-haul (1,500-3,500km):** GBP 350
- **Long-haul (over 3,500km):** GBP 520 (50% reduction if delay is 3-4 hours)

Extraordinary circumstances include severe weather, air traffic control restrictions, security incidents, and political instability. In these cases, compensation does not apply, but assistance and rerouting are still provided.

## Cancellations

If SkyLine Airways cancels your flight, you are entitled to choose between:

1. A full refund to the original payment method
2. Rebooking on the next available SkyLine Airways flight at no extra cost
3. Rerouting on a partner airline at SkyLine Airways' expense

Compensation under UK261/EU261 applies if notification is given less than 14 days before departure, subject to the same extraordinary circumstances exemption.

## Missed connections

If a SkyLine Airways flight delay causes a missed connection on a SkyLine Airways or partner ticket, we will rebook you on the next available connecting flight at no charge. This applies only when both flights are on the same booking reference.
