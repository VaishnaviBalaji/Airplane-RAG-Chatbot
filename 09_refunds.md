# Refunds Policy

**Document ID:** POL-REF-001
**Last updated:** 2026-02-25

## Eligible refunds

You are entitled to a full refund in the following circumstances:

- SkyLine Airways cancels your flight and you choose not to be rebooked
- Your flight is delayed by more than 5 hours and you choose not to travel
- A close family member (immediate family only) passes away within 30 days of departure (medical certificate required)
- Government-issued travel restrictions prevent travel

Partial refunds may apply for voluntary cancellations depending on fare type. See the Booking Changes and Cancellations policy for fare-specific rules.

## Processing time

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

- **Credit/debit card:** 7-14 working days
- **Bank transfer:** 14-21 working days
- **Travel voucher (where applicable):** Issued within 48 hours

If you have not received your refund within the stated timeframe, please contact your card issuer or bank, as processing delays are sometimes outside SkyLine Airways' control.

## Vouchers vs cash refunds

In some cases, SkyLine Airways may offer a travel voucher in lieu of a cash refund. Vouchers:

- Are valued at 110% of the original ticket value
- Are valid for 24 months from issue
- Can be used for flights, baggage, or seat selection
- Cannot be transferred or exchanged for cash

Acceptance of a voucher is voluntary; passengers always retain the right to a cash refund where eligible.

## Non-refundable items

The following are not refundable under any circumstances:

- Saver fare tickets (taxes only refundable)
- Booking fees
- Seat selection fees (unless flight is cancelled)
- Travel insurance (refer to your insurance provider)

## How to request a refund

Refund requests can be submitted via the Manage My Booking section of the SkyLine Airways website, or by contacting customer services. You will need your booking reference and the email address used at booking.
