# SkyMiles Loyalty Programme

**Document ID:** POL-LOY-001
**Last updated:** 2026-03-01

## Tier overview

SkyLine Airways' SkyMiles programme has four tiers:

- **Blue:** Entry tier, free to join.
- **Silver:** Achieved with 25,000 tier miles or 30 flights in a calendar year.
- **Gold:** Achieved with 50,000 tier miles or 60 flights in a calendar year.
- **Platinum:** Achieved with 100,000 tier miles or 120 flights in a calendar year.

Tier status is valid for 12 months from the month it is earned.

## Earning miles

Miles are earned on all SkyLine Airways and partner flights, based on fare class:

- **Saver:** 25% of distance flown
- **Standard:** 100% of distance flown
- **Flex:** 150% of distance flown
- **Business:** 200% of distance flown
- **First:** 300% of distance flown

Bonus miles apply for Silver (25%), Gold (50%), and Platinum (100%) members.

## Redeeming miles

Miles can be redeemed for:

- Reward flights on SkyLine Airways and partner airlines
- Cabin upgrades (subject to availability)
- Excess baggage fees
- Lounge access for non-eligible members

Reward flight redemptions start from 8,000 miles for short-haul economy and 30,000 miles for long-haul economy. Taxes and surcharges still apply.

## Tier benefits

| Benefit | Blue | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Priority check-in | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Lounge access | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Extra checked bag | No | +1 | +1 | +2 |
| Priority boarding | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Guaranteed seat | No | No | No | Yes |
| Companion lounge access | No | No | 1 guest | 2 guests |

## Mile expiry

Miles expire after 24 months of account inactivity. Any earning or redemption activity resets the expiry clock.
