# Carry-On Baggage Policy

**Document ID:** POL-BAG-001
**Last updated:** 2026-01-15

## Allowance by cabin class

All SkyLine Airways passengers are entitled to bring carry-on baggage into the cabin, subject to the following limits:

- **Economy:** One cabin bag (max 7kg) plus one personal item (max 3kg).
- **Premium Economy:** One cabin bag (max 10kg) plus one personal item (max 3kg).
- **Business:** Two cabin bags (max 10kg each) plus one personal item (max 3kg).
- **First:** Two cabin bags (max 12kg each) plus one personal item (max 3kg).

## Size restrictions

The cabin bag must not exceed 55cm x 40cm x 23cm (including wheels and handles). The personal item must fit under the seat in front of you and not exceed 40cm x 30cm x 15cm.

A personal item is defined as a handbag, laptop bag, small backpack, or similar small item.

## Items not permitted in carry-on

- Liquids over 100ml in individual containers
- Sharp objects including scissors with blades over 6cm
- Lithium batteries above 100Wh without prior approval
- Sporting equipment (see separate sports equipment policy)

## Gate-checked bags

If the cabin is full, SkyLine Airways reserves the right to gate-check carry-on bags free of charge. Gate-checked bags will be returned at the destination baggage carousel.
