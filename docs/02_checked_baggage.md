# Checked Baggage Policy

**Document ID:** POL-BAG-002
**Last updated:** 2026-02-03

## Standard allowance

Checked baggage allowance depends on cabin class and route:

- **Economy (short-haul):** 1 bag, max 23kg.
- **Economy (long-haul):** 2 bags, max 23kg each.
- **Premium Economy:** 2 bags, max 23kg each.
- **Business:** 2 bags, max 32kg each.
- **First:** 3 bags, max 32kg each.

Short-haul is defined as flights under 4 hours; long-haul is 4 hours or longer.

## Excess baggage fees

Bags exceeding the included allowance are charged as follows:

- Additional bag: GBP 60 (short-haul) / GBP 120 (long-haul)
- Overweight bag (24-32kg): GBP 50
- Oversized bag (sum of dimensions over 158cm): GBP 80

Bags over 32kg cannot be checked and must be shipped as cargo.

## Prohibited items

The following must not be placed in checked baggage:

- Lithium batteries and power banks (must travel in carry-on)
- Cash, jewellery, electronics over GBP 500 in value
- Medication and travel documents
- Fragile or perishable items (SkyLine Airways accepts no liability)

## Lost or delayed baggage

Report missing baggage at the SkyLine Airways desk in the arrivals hall before leaving the airport. Compensation for delayed baggage is GBP 50 per day (up to 3 days) for essential purchases. Lost baggage compensation follows the Montreal Convention, capped at approximately GBP 1,300 per passenger.
