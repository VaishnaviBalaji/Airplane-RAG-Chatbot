# Online Check-In and Boarding

**Document ID:** POL-CHK-001
**Last updated:** 2026-03-10

## Online check-in window

Online check-in opens 48 hours before scheduled departure for all flights and closes:

- **Short-haul:** 60 minutes before departure
- **Long-haul:** 90 minutes before departure

Online check-in is available via the SkyLine Airways website and mobile app. Passengers who check in online may print their boarding pass or use a mobile boarding pass.

## Airport check-in cutoffs

For passengers checking in at the airport:

- **Short-haul (with checked baggage):** 45 minutes before departure
- **Short-haul (carry-on only):** 30 minutes before departure
- **Long-haul (any baggage):** 60 minutes before departure

Passengers arriving after the cutoff will not be accepted for travel and will be subject to the no-show policy.

## Boarding gates

Boarding typically begins 30 minutes before departure for short-haul flights and 45 minutes before departure for long-haul flights. Gates close 15 minutes before scheduled departure. Passengers not at the gate by gate closure will be denied boarding.

## Seat selection

Seat selection is available during online check-in. Standard seats are free for Standard, Flex, Business, and First fare passengers. Saver fare passengers can select a seat for GBP 8 (short-haul) or GBP 18 (long-haul). Extra-legroom seats are available for purchase from GBP 15.
