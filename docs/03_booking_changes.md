# Booking Changes and Cancellations

**Document ID:** POL-BOOK-001
**Last updated:** 2026-01-22

## Cancellation by passenger

Cancellation policies depend on the fare type purchased:

- **Saver:** Non-refundable. Taxes only refundable on request.
- **Standard:** Refundable up to 24 hours before departure, with a GBP 50 cancellation fee.
- **Flex:** Fully refundable up to 1 hour before departure with no fee.
- **Business and First (any fare):** Fully refundable up to 1 hour before departure.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-14 working days.

## Date and time changes

Date changes are permitted under the following conditions:

- **Saver:** Changes not permitted. Passengers must cancel and rebook.
- **Standard:** GBP 75 change fee plus any fare difference.
- **Flex:** No change fee, fare difference applies.
- **Business and First:** No change fee, fare difference applies.

Changes must be made at least 2 hours before the original scheduled departure.

## Name changes

Minor spelling corrections (up to 3 characters) are free of charge. Full name changes are not permitted; the booking must be cancelled and rebooked under the correct name. Passengers should contact SkyLine Airways directly for name corrections.

## 24-hour grace period

All bookings made directly through SkyLine Airways are eligible for free cancellation within 24 hours of booking, provided the flight is at least 7 days away.
