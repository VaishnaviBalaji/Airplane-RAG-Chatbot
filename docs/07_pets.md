# Pet Travel Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PET-001
**Last updated:** 2026-02-12

## Pets in the cabin

Small dogs and cats may travel in the cabin on most SkyLine Airways flights, subject to the following:

- Combined weight of pet and carrier must not exceed 8kg
- Carrier dimensions: max 45cm x 30cm x 25cm
- Carrier must be soft-sided, leak-proof, and well-ventilated
- Pet must remain in the carrier under the seat in front for the entire flight
- Maximum 4 pets in the cabin per flight (first-come, first-served)

Cabin pet fee: GBP 50 (short-haul) or GBP 100 (long-haul) per direction.

## Pets in the hold

Pets exceeding cabin limits travel in the climate-controlled hold:

- Carrier must be IATA-compliant rigid plastic
- Pet must be able to stand, turn, and lie down naturally
- Hold pet fee: GBP 150 (short-haul) or GBP 300 (long-haul) per direction

## Restrictions

The following pets cannot travel on SkyLine Airways flights:

- Snub-nosed breeds (e.g. pug, bulldog, Persian cat) due to respiratory risk
- Pets under 16 weeks old
- Pregnant pets in the third trimester
- Aggressive or distressed animals at the discretion of ground crew

## Documentation

All pets require:

- Up-to-date vaccinations including rabies (administered at least 21 days prior)
- A pet passport or animal health certificate
- Microchip identification compliant with ISO standard 11784/11785

Some destinations have additional import requirements. Passengers are responsible for ensuring all paperwork is in order.

## Assistance dogs

Recognised assistance dogs travel free of charge in the cabin and are exempt from carrier requirements. See the Special Assistance policy for full details.
